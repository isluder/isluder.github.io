I'm a Ph.D. student in Mechanical Engineering focused on composite materials, 3D printing, and machine learning applications in materials science ♻️.

I study under my Professor, Dr. Kwek Tze Tan, at the Advanced Metacomposite Laboratory at the University of Akron 🎓. His website is here:

My research focuses on materials science, currently that is environmental effects 💧 on 3D-printed composites. I developed a custom sensor system 🧑‍💻 to continuously measure temperature, humidity, and pressure to analyze water diffusion in 3D-printed nylon-based composites reinforced with chopped carbon fibers. These materials are widely used but suffer strength degradation in high-moisture environments. I hope to develope predictive models by integrating an ESP32 microcontroller with environmental sensors and collecting mass measurement.

In the future, I hope to do research in Impact Damage 💥 and Fatigue. Topic is TBD, but I have special interest in Tribology, Vibrations, and Lattice Structures.

Beyond this project, I am interested in running 🏃, coding, videogames 👾, and making the best cup of espresso ☕️. Check out my website for some of my related and unrelated projects.
